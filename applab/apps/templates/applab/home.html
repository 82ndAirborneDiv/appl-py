Mobile Application Prototypes (AppLab)

AppLab is a tool for the Informatics Innovation Unit(IIU), CDC, and its public health partners to test and collaborate on innovative mobile applications and prototypes. AppLab features apps for iOS and Android. Users must follow our code of conduct.

Every app has a page which provides an overview / summary and will often include screenshots. The bottom of the overview page will display the versions of the app currently available for download. Every app will have at least one version to download. For most users, we recommend downloading the most recent version (top of the list).

Please email informaticslab@cdc.gov with any comments or questions.
